range():
-------
>it always generate sequence of int elements

range(arg):
----------
	>start	->0
	>stop	->arg
	>step	->+1

range(arg1,arg2):
----------------
	>start	->arg1
	>stop	->arg2
	>step	->+1

range(arg1,arg2,arg3):
----------------
	>start	->arg1
	>stop	->arg2
	>step	->arg3

start---->inclusive
stop----->exclusive
arg3----->must not be zero
arg1,arg2,arg3----->must be int
